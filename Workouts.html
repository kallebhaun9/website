Workouts You Can Do With Little To No Equipment

Boot Camp

After you have enlisted, you will be shipped out to boot camp where you will undergo intense training to prepare you for the day you see combat. This article's purpose is to give you various workouts that will make you stronger before boot camp arrives. They are also just very intense workouts for anyone looking to build strength without going to a gym.

The Workouts

Workout 1

Jog 3 miles
Do 5 sets of 20 pushups, crunches, squats, dips, and burpees

Workout 2

Jog 1 mile.
Do 2 minutes of pushups then 2 minutes of situps.
Jog another mile.
Stay in pushup position for 1 minute.
Do 6 sets of 5 pullups, jumping squats, and pushups. (Sprint across your lawn between each set)
Do jumping jacks for 3 minutes.
Jog another mile.

Workout 3

Jog 1 mile.
Do 5 sets of 15 squats, burpees, and pushups.
Do 2 minutes of situps.
Run 1 mile.
Do 6 sets of 10 squats, 3 pullups, and 10 lunges on each leg with sprinting across your lawn between each set.

Workout 4

Run back and forth across your yard and do 1 burpree.
Run back and forth again then do 2 burpees.
Run again and do 3.
Repeat the circuit until you get to 20.
